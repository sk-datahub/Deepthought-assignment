# Methodology

## Scope
City: Pune  
Segment: Biotech / Diagnostics / Life Sciences  

## Approach
I searched for companies using Google, LinkedIn, and company websites. I created an initial list of around 50–60 companies.

## Filtering
- Removed service companies (CROs, consulting firms)
- Removed companies above 500 Cr revenue
- Focused on manufacturing companies in biotech and diagnostics

## AI Usage
AI was used as an assistant to understand the scoring framework (C1–C6) and structure the analysis. Final decisions were made manually.

## Validation
- Checked company websites
- Verified manufacturing presence
- Corrected AI mistakes

## Learning
Some companies initially appeared correct but were later rejected after deeper validation.
