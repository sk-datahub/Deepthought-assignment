# 1000 Company Proposal

## Plan
To build 1000 ICP companies, I would start with 3000–4000 companies and filter them.

## Sources
- DSIR R&D list
- Expo exhibitor lists
- BSE SME companies
- LinkedIn
- MCA database

## Process
1. Collect large dataset
2. Apply filtering (manufacturing, revenue)
3. Use AI for scoring
4. Manual verification
5. Final 1000 companies

## Timeline
Week 1: Data collection  
Week 2: Scoring  
Week 3: Validation  
Week 4: Final list  

## Quality Control
- Cross-check websites
- Remove wrong companies
- Validate manually
